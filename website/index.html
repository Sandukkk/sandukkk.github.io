About Me

I received my B.E. in Computer Engineering in 2025 from the Institute of Engineering, Pulchowk Campus, Tribhuvan University . My research interest lies in machine learning, computer vision, privacy-preserving ML, multimodal learning, and generative AI, with an emphasis on robustness and trustworthy systems.

I received the Best Capstone Project Award 2025 from the IOE Department of Electronics and Computer Engineering for my research on Handwritten Devanagari Character Recognition: Dataset Benchmarking with Transfer Learning. I was also recognized as a top data science student by the Samsung Innovation Campus. My academic work has been published in the 17th IAPR International Workshop on Document Analysis Systems and the 4th World Conference on Information Systems for Business Management 2025.

I currently conduct industry research at a fintech company, working at the intersection of machine learning and financial security, with behaviour analysis and time-series predictions. I aspire to keep advancing privacy-preserving machine learning and computer vision.

Projects

  • Character Recognition of Nepali Vehicle Number Plate

    A robust two-stage license-plate detection and recognition pipeline for real-world Nepali transportation data, handling diverse lighting, non-standardized formats, and the Devanagari script. I implemented the deep CNNs and used transfer learning to adapt SOTA architectures for Nepali characters, with YOLOv8 for plate localization and PyTorch for the recognition models.

    Code Paper

  • Handwritten Devanagari Character Recognition

    A novel dataset targeting handwritten Devanagari conjunct consonants, which are under-represented in existing datasets. I led the data-collection protocol and dataset cleaning, then applied transfer learning with deep CNNs in PyTorch to establish baseline performance metrics.

    Code

  • Artifuse: Image Inpainting with AI

    An end-to-end image inpainting system that restores missing or corrupted regions with perceptually accurate results. I designed and trained conditional GANs (adapting Pix2Pix) to synthesize missing pixels, and integrated the PyTorch pipelines into a full-stack web deployment for real-time, interactive inference.

    Code
